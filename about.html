About Us

The Arcade is a hobby project, designed to be a silly little outlet for things I find neat, eg retro games, maybe I'll add a calculator or something, just general 'coding projects' wrapped together in a neat little site. The Arcade's name was inspired by the arcades of ye olden days, when you had to go up to a cabinet and select a game to play, similair to how you just select a page to go to.
